Don't Wait. Save a Life Today

Because of You, Life Doesn’t Stop

Every 10 minutes, someone in Mauritius needs blood.
This could be a little Thalassemic girl in the ICU or a mother with Stage 3 leukemia or even a son who just met with an accident.
Unseen emergencies happen every day.

Don’t wait to save life.

And if you’re worried about needles, don’t be — most blood donors compare the experience to a mild, split-second pinch!
The entire process is very safe and very fast, and you will feel amazing knowing you potentially saved up to three people.

The Blood Donors Association is a NGO that supports health authorities and patients to promote and organise safe and sustainable blood programmes.

Over the last 20 years, we have supported the collection of over 500,000 pints of blood.
